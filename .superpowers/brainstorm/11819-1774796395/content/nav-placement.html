Where does the Cross-Clip Dashboard live?

The dashboard aggregates data across all clips. Where should it sit in the nav?

A

Top-level tab — "Dashboard" or "Analytics"

Sits alongside Clips, Characters, Queue etc. in the main nav. Always accessible, treated as a first-class view.

B

Inside the Kanban / Episode Tracker

The Kanban already shows clip statuses — analytics lives as a sub-tab there. Keeps production and analytics co-located.

C

Floating panel / overlay

Accessible from a button anywhere in the app. Doesn't take up a nav slot — pops over the current view.
